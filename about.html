About

Hello, I'm Matt.

I'm a founder and full-stack developer building practical software at the intersection of finance, AI, and everyday life. My work includes P3 Lending Protocol, Pilot, Relay, and other products designed to make complex technology feel useful, trustworthy, and human.

Right now, I'm focused on building responsible financial technology, developer tools, and AI-powered products that give people more control over their money, work, and digital lives.
